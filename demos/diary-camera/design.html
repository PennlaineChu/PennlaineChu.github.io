Acoustic Design Notes (INMP441)

Concern Solution
Port alignment 1.0-1.5mm acoustic port aligned with MEMS inlet
Sealed back cavity Gasket ring (closed-cell silicone/TPU)
Wind/plosive noise Acoustic mesh (stainless or sintered PTFE)
Vibration isolation Soft standoffs or silicone pad
Speaker crosstalk Mic & speaker on opposite faces (>20mm separation)
Port tube length Keep ≤2mm (wall thickness) to avoid Helmholtz resonance

Design Specs

  • Style: "Soft pebble" / luxury compact — no boxy edges
  • Wall thickness: 2-3mm
  • Top surface: Kailh Box shutter (centered, proud recess) + SPDT slide switch (rear)
  • Bottom edge: USB-C port (TP4056 charge)
  • Bottom face: Speaker grille
  • Internal: Z-axis sandwich — WS2812 ring + camera → MCU/battery → speaker
