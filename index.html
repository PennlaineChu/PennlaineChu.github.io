Diary Camera

A multifunction camera and journaling app for girls like me — drawn to bullet journaling and photo dumps, but rarely reflected in the tools we're given. An IoT camera uploads photos and text to a digital diary, with algorithmic layouts that auto-compose memory pages. Explore the interactive progress tracker.
